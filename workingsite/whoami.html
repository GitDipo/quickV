Who Am I?

Verifiers

You are a third party that needs employment or salary verifications for your clients. You may be a bank, a mortgage company, or even a potential employer.

Employees

You are a present or past employee of a Quick Confirm client. Using Quick Confirm enables you to view your personal information or authorize a salary verification for a third party verifier.

Employer

You are a Quick Confirm client with administrative rights. You can run various reports.

If you want to automate verifications for your employees, Answer Our Brief RFI and we will contact you to explain the benefits of using Quick Confirm.

Social Services

Please provide a fax cover sheet including your permissable purpose, client’s authorization, and your return fax number.
