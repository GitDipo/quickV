Ordering a Researched Verification

  • Enter all fields on the Verifier Research Request Page
  • You need a minimum of $50.00 in your Prepay account, press prepay to add funds
  • Upload a signed authorization form for salary information or use our release form, click on the link Release Of Information Form
  • Use the instructions box to provide additional details
  • Press Submit

Viewing Requests

  • Press Request Details

Editing Requests

  • You can correct email addresses or phone numbers
  • View progress
  • Add comments or list additional documents required
  • Use comments to cancel open/incomplete requests (Only requests that are not yet in progress can be cancelled)
  • Retrieve your completed verification
