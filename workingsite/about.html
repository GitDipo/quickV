About Us

We're here to provide you with the very best service for all your employment verification needs.

QuickConfirm.com is a leading employment and salary verification service provider offering secure, state of the art solutions for verifiers, social service agencies, employees, and employers.

In addition to secure employment and salary verifications, QuickConfirm provides electronic W2s, Immigration Letters, and Unemployment Compensation services. QuickConfirm partners will leading providers of complementary services to extend and enhance our services and bring well rounded, professional services to our clients.

QuickConfirm has been providing verification services for for nearly two decades. In that time, we have worked continuously to provide secure, reliable and accurate fulfillment of all requests.

QuickConfirm does not permit blind searches of our database and never distributes any data. Data belongs to our clients and their employees and is always handled with their privacy and security in mind.
